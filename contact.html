Frequently Asked Questions

How can I borrow a book?

+

To borrow a book, you first need to create an account in the Digital Library. Then, you can browse the catalog and select the book you want to borrow and click on the "Borrow" button. The borrowing period is 14 days and can be renewed once if there is no waiting list for the book.

What is the borrowing period?

+

The basic borrowing period is 14 days. You can renew the borrowing for an additional 14 days if there is no waiting list for the book. You can check due dates in the "My Borrowings" page.

How can I renew a book borrowing?

+

You can renew borrowing from the "My Borrowings" page by clicking on the "Renew" button next to the book you want to renew. You can only renew once per book, provided there is no waiting list for the book.

Can I download books for offline reading?

+

Yes, you can download most available books for offline reading. After borrowing, you will find a "Download" option on the book page. Some books may be restricted to platform-only reading due to publisher restrictions.

What happens if I'm late in returning a book?

+

In case of late book return, your account will be temporarily suspended from making new borrowings until the book is returned. We recommend adhering to return dates to avoid any account suspension.

Can I suggest a book to be added to the library?

+

Yes, we welcome book suggestions. You can use the contact form and select "Book Suggestion" as the message subject. We will study the suggestion and add it to the library if it is available for electronic publishing.
